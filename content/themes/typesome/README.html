Configuring

Before turning on your theme live, you should probably set up some of the functions you want to use.

Note: Most configurable files are located in typesome/partials/custom/ folder.

I. Various metatags - meta.hbs

Open meta.hbs file with your text editor and enter your data in the fields you want to use.

For example:

  • [twitter:site] Used for Twitter Card identification, the twitter @username of the owner of this card's domain. (read more)
  • [twitter:creator] Used for Twitter Card identification, the twitter @username of the author of this content. (read more)
  • [google-site-verification] Used for Google Webmaster Tools identification. (read more)
  • [fb:admins] Used for Facebook Insights identification. (read more)

II. Disqus comments - default.hbs & post.hbs

To connect Disqus commenting system, open default.hbs and post.hbs files with your text editor. Now find and replace Disqus shortname - (example) with your own and save the files. (read more) or see Ghost Installation Instructions for Disqus

III. Google Analytics - google_analytics.hbs

To connect Google Analytics, simply open google_analytics.hbs file with your text editor and replace (UA-XXXXXX-XX) with your own Google Analytics ID and save the file. (read more)

IV. Social media icons - footer.hbs

To add your social media links in footer, simply open footer.hbs file with your text editor, paste your links in the right place and save the file.

For example:

  • Link for Twitter profile in href=".." quotation marks

Note: You can add a different footer icons, by using css classes (like: fa-twitter for example) from Font Awesome, which are included.

Installing

  • Copy the unziped Typesome folder into your Ghost theme directory ghost/content/themes
  • Restart Ghost and log in to your dashboard.
  • In settings under themes select Typesome and save.
  • Done, your new theme is now up and runnig.
